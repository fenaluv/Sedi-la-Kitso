Multimeter Basics — Measure Voltage, Continuity and Resistance

Published: 2022-01-14 — By Fena

A multimeter is one of the most useful tools you’ll own. This guide covers safe use, common settings, and simple checks every beginner should know.

What is a multimeter?

It measures voltage (AC/DC), current, and resistance (ohms). Some models test continuity and diode checks — useful for identifying broken wires or components.
